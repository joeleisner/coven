Coven Playground

Welcome to the Coven Playground! This is a space for experimenting with web components and sharing your creations with others.

To get started, you can create a new web component by defining a class that extends HTMLElement and registering it with a custom tag name. You can then use your custom element in your HTML just like any other element.

Feel free to explore the various features of web components, such as shadow DOM, custom events, and lifecycle callbacks. You can also share your components with the community by submitting them to the registry.
